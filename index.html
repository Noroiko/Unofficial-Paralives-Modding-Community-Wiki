A public, community-built knowledge base

This wiki started life as a shared spreadsheet — a living troubleshooting guide written by the people actually making mods for Paralives. It is run by the modders of the Paralives Discord, mainly managed by TheParaGrimm, and all help is appreciated.

Everything here is community-sourced. If you've solved a problem, found a great tutorial, or discovered something new, your contribution is welcome.

Contact: DM @grimmdrandom on Discord for queries.
