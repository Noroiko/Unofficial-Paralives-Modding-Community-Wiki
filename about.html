About & Credits

A public, community-built knowledge base for modding Paralives — and the people who make it happen.

What this is

The Unofficial Paralives Modding Community Wiki began life as a shared, searchable spreadsheet — a living troubleshooting guide written by the people actually making mods for Paralives. This site is that same knowledge, reorganised into an easy-to-browse format.

It is a public resource. Everything here is community-sourced, and you're welcome to add your findings, information & pictures. It is run by the modders of the Paralives Discord, mainly managed by TheParaGrimm — all help is appreciated!

How to contribute

  • Share answers, tutorials, tools and tips in the community Discord.
  • Have a fix for an open question? Post it — confirmed findings get written up on the Q&A page.
  • Interaction mods aren't well covered yet. If you'd like to help document them, please do!
  • The “Added by” credit on resources is entirely optional 💜

Get in touch

DM @grimmdrandom on Discord for queries — or if you need logos.
